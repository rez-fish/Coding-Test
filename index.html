Welcome to Renewable Forest Products

Our experience is second to none with Andrew Van Riper on board. Andy has over 25 years of custom grinding experience to include 2 hurricane clean ups, Fran and Bertha. From roadway clearing, for example the 485 beltline in Charlotte, NC, to countless landfills, golf courses, municipalities and even destroying Caterpillar foundry forms, we can likely handle your project. With many screen sizes available, we are sure to provide you with the right product.

Presently, we operate a Morbark 6600 with 1125 HP, and a Morbark 4600 with 630 HP. We have the support equipment to do it all ourselves, or we can allow you to load the grinder. Contact us for a quote, or to discuss how we can serve your needs.

Renewable Forest products was birthed in 2019 with a vision to provide superior/exceptional service in the Custom grinding industry in Wisconsin and bordering states.
